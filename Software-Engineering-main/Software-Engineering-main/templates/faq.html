Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a complaint?

Click “File Complaint” in the navigation bar and fill out the short form.

How are drivers matched to trucks?

Each truck number is assigned to a specific driver. The system automatically groups complaints by both driver and truck for clarity.

⚠️ Why are some drivers or trucks flagged?

If two or more complaints are submitted for the same truck, the system marks that truck and its driver as "Flagged" to indicate repeated issues.

What is the summary report?

The summary report gives a high-level view of total complaints, driver activity, and complaint statuses, making it easy for supervisors to review patterns.
