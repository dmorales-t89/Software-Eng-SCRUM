About This System

The Complaint Tracking System is designed to help civilians report unsafe or unprofessional truck driving behavior quickly and easily. Our system organizes complaints by drivers and trucks, highlights repeated issues, and gives supervisors a clear overview of company safety.

What This System Does

  • Accepts public complaints through a quick submission form
  • Automatically organizes complaints by driver and truck
  • Flags trucks and drivers with repeated issues
  • Provides a clean summary report for supervisors

Our Goal

The goal of this system is to improve road safety by ensuring complaints are documented, reviewed, and resolved in a consistent and transparent manner.
